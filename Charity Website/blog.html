Jamshoro Charitable Organization's mission is to help the people who are in need who are not capable of helping themselves. We want to raise awarness, inspire, and encourage people to take meaningful actions, in the form of donation that contibute to overreaching our goals. By providing images, real life stories we try to get positive outcomes of their contributions.
our single effort may brings clean water and safe drinking, Medicine, homes to the homeless, education to the students who want to get education but unable due to their financial conditions, high tuition fees, or some other reasons.
Our vision is to become inspiration for those who are afraid to reach out the people. We want a society where every one has equal opportunities, rights and access of reasources, regardless of background or circumstances.
Currently we are working on the projects like lets Not Leave Alone That Can't afford Education and people who are fighting hunger battles. we have volunteers to interact with the peolpe who doesn't have any reasources.
