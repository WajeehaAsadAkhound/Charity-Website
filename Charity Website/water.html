WATER AID PROJECT

By understanding, water for people is more than just a foundation for drinking, for satisfying thirst, Water is beyond this. Water play vital role in various domains of life such as Health, Supporting cleanliness, Empowering womens, facilitating education, promoting livelihoods and Communty development. Water for people adopts a system thinking that consider water in the context of sanitation and hygiene practices, and overall being.
People feelings, emotions and perceptions about water can vary based on their Experiences, Background, Locations and the impact on their daily life due to the lack of access of clean water. A person can deals with tons of emotions like frustration, Anxiety, Helplessness and struggles they faces to accomplish their daily life task because their basic needs are not completed.
We designed Water Aid Project by considering the emotions, the struggles people are facing, Water Aid Project not only addresses the practical aspects of water access but also the emotional and psychological of well-being individuals and communities. With the help of community members and local Goverment and our partners we take initiatives that empowers and involve communties in finding sustainable water solutions that contribute to restoring a sense of dignity and hope for those facing water challenges.

Sanitation And Hygiene


As globally the papulation increases the need to clean water is increases, the opportunity to access to clean water for sanitation and hygiene remains a challenge globally. For every human, Clean water is one of its fundamental right and play a important role in preventing the spread of disease and promoting over all health. It also has a significant impact on education. Insufficient availability of suitable resources like water, sanitation, and hygiene facilities can lower the attendance and educational achievement in schools. Children who suffer from diseases like waterborne illnesses or who have lack access to toilets may miss school or drop out altogether. Aim of our project isto enable children to go to school more often and learn better in a cleaner, healthier environment. The reason people faces difficulties in sanitation and hygiene is due to Contamination of water in distribution systems, Growing water scarcity and Climate changes. The ultimate solutions are to investments in infrastructures and sanitation facilities Hygiene education. Involvement of women and men in managing water and sanitation resources, Addressing the inequal distribution of water. Inovation of technologies to recycle the water.

Rain Water Harvesting System


The idea of rain water harvesting comes from the ideato take advantage from the free resource, to capture the free water falling on our roofs. We take initiative of rain water harvesting system because we believe that it is very useful technology and play a vital role in development of our societies, city and country. Basically, Rain water harvesting starts from collecting rain water from the roofs. Then implement a storage system like barrels, tanks at the downspout of your roof gutter system. The rain water is channeled into gutters, then into the downspout and finally in the barrels. The water in the storage system should pass through filtration system to remove and make sure that their will be no any harmful remaining pieces. Harvested water can be used for various purposes like washing machine, garden irrigation and even for non potable uses around the house. By implememting harvesting system you can take control of your water supply and replace generous portion of your water needs.

Women Empowerment Program


In many developing countries, the responsiblity of collecting water reserved for womens and girls, which can be time-consuming task. There will be alot of loss in time which they can spend on their education. 72% of household water are carried by women proved by researches. According to their point of view their regular life has been affected due to carrying large amount of water for long distances. Having access to clean water nearby allows them to spend less time on water collection and more time on their education, pursing job opportunities, and taking care of their domestic duties. With more power of education they can break the cycle of poverty and contributingto their Empowerment and resilience, especially in the face of water scarcity and climate change. They can engage in small bussiness, Community engagement so they can be economic well-being of their families and societies.

Emergency Response Kit


Water plays a key role in emergency response kits, serving various purposes to address immediate needs during disasters or emergencies. IN any emergency situation or any crisis water is a primary concern. Emergency response kits often include water purification tablets, filters, or pre-packaged water to ensure individuals have a reliable source of potable water, especially when the regular water supply may be compromised. Staying hydrated is essential for overall health, especially during stressful and challenging situations. Dehydration can exacerbate the impact of emergencies, so having an adequate supply of water in emergency kits helps maintain individuals' health and well-being. For First aid, Wound cleaning and injuries the basic necessity is water. Sterile water or saline solution is often included in emergency kits to clean wounds, helping prevent infection and promoting faster healing. In situations, especially where individuals may be exposed to extreme heat, Water can be used for cooling purposes. In hot climates or during heatwaves, having access to water can help prevent heat-related illnesses. Including an adequate supply of water and water-related items in emergency response kits ensures that basic hydration, hygiene, and health requirements are met, contributing to the overall effectiveness of emergency response efforts.
