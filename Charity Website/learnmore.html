lets Not Leave Alone That Can't afford Education

Within a stage where the importance of knowledge and skills are increasing rapidly. Education is what that teaches us how to interact and most important it humantizes us. In this Era lets not forget who are not able to avail education. In this world full of opportunities, Education is basic need, regardless every child is not lucky. Some because of their background, other because of poverty and other prevented just because they are girls.
The primary hardle faced by those unable to afford education is financial barrier. With rising tution fees, Expensive text books, and additional costs related with schooling. Inequality in education is something that no child should suffer. Every child has right to secure their own future through education. The stories of those who can't afford education are not just individual narratives; they are reflections of systemic issues that demand collective attention and action. Girls education is somethingthat is not only important for their ownselves but is important for families. One educated girl mean one educated family. A educated girl deliver her education to child.
While world is celebraating literacy rate pakistan has nothing to celebrate. According to federal ministry report estimated papulation of 60 million is illiterate in the country. A significant portion of the global population is forced to watch from sidelines as other progress academically.
Our aim to help students eho had to leave to education from not going to school by providing scholarships, textbooks, equipments, school buildings and accessibility for disabled. We signup with the people who helps us in this process to provide education to affected peole.
